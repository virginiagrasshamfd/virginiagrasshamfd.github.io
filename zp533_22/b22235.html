評　価90点
Tasted ex-chateau and single blind in Southwold. The Clos du Marquis is an excellent Saint Julien from Mon. Delon, although it did not quite meet my expectations under blind conditions. It has a refined, well-defined bouquet with blackberry, cedar and Margaux-like violets. The aromatics are fresh and vibrant with well-integrated oak. The palate is medium-bodied and succulent, fleshy and generous with a sweet, velvety finish. Very satisfying, though it is perhaps not a long term Saint Julien. Tasted January 2012.
Wine Journal #0312 （2012-03-31）
