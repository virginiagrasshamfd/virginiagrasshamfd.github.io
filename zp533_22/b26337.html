説明:

The ENHANCE Infiltrate KL1 offers pro level gaming features for gamers of all skill levels. A modern design with elegant LED accents and a membrane keyboard switch design make the Infiltrate KL1 an ideal choice for the digital battlegrounds.

The heavy duty braided cable connects directly to your computers USB port, and begins working right away, with Plug and Play technology.  Switch between a dynamic LED breathing mode that pulses rhythmically or select the static multi-color mode for a static color filled display. Quiet membrane switches are great for late night gaming so you don"t wake up your roommates or partner with loud or clacky keys. The Infiltrate KL1 is equipped with Anti-Ghosting keys to prevent unintended key presses and also features 19 Key Rollover to extend the reliability and accuracy of every key press. Choose from 3 brightness settings that affect the LED backlighting and the side panel accents to get the perfect lighting level for your computer desk.
