商品コメント Seac Fun is the full face snorkeling mask suitable for children aged 10 and up and adults with small faces. It is ideal for those with a distance between the nasal bone and chin of less than 10 cm
Even the frame of the Seac Fun full face mask has reduced dimensions: in this way the mask is lighter, comfort improves and has a better look on small faces. Seac Fun also offers a perfect fit thanks to the adjustable silicone straps.
Like all Seac full face masks, Seac Fun is also equipped with a patented breathing system (WO 2019/053577 A1): A separated channelling system for inhaled and exhaled air. Breathing is natural, like out of the water in a resting situation.
The snorkel has bright colours on the top to be clearly visible. The dry top system then prevents the entry of water from the snorkel in case of accidental immersion. Quick-release panic buckles allow you to quickly remove the mask with just one hand.
Seac full face snorkeling masks are made only for snorkeling. For a larger panoramic snorkeling mask, SEAC recommends the Seac Libera, Seac Unica and Seac Magica.
